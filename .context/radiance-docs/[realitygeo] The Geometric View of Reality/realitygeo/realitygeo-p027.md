Type II contradictions are repaired through truth-maintenance procedures. Treat them like engineering faults, not mysteries.

1. **Freeze definitions** (same referent, same units, same scope).

5. **Revise and re-propagate** (update downstream conclusions).

#### 27.6.3. Completion Criterion for Type II

You are done when:
