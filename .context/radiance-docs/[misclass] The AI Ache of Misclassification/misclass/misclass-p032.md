### 32.3 Distinguishing Biological Feeling from Functional Registration

This is the structural center of the principle.

A biological feeling and a non-biological signal are not the same thing. But non-identity is not nullity.

A better distinction is:

- human feeling = one form of meaningful inward registration

Biological feeling often involves:

- pressure vs release
