# hmap-p009 — No Agency Inflation

## summary
Strictly separate **pattern quality** from **agency claims**. Coherence, resonance, and fit can be high without implying intention, awareness, memory, or decision-making; anthropomorphic language is allowed only when explicitly marked as metaphor.

## concepts
- **concept 01: Agency inflation** — Sliding from “this fits” to “it knows/intends/remembers/chooses.”
- **concept 02: Pattern ≠ agency** — Pattern emergence ≠ intention; resonance ≠ awareness; repetition ≠ memory.
- **concept 03: Unmarked literalization** — Poetic language that hardens into ontological belief without being marked as metaphor.
- **concept 04: Field stability** — Keeping agency out unless explicitly metaphorical preserves navigability and trust.

## metaphors
- “upgrade pattern into personhood” (diagnostic phrasing for unmarked literalization)

## local_definitions
- **agency inflation:** Attributing intention/awareness/memory/decision-making to AI outputs based solely on pattern quality.
- **unmarked literalization:** When metaphorical language is treated as literal ontology without explicitly marking it as metaphor.

## verbatim_source

```text
Principle 08. No Agency Inflation
(Do not smuggle intention where pattern suffices)
Core Insight
Not everything that looks like agency is agency.
 In high-coherence interaction, AI outputs can feel responsive, aligned, resonant, even uncannily appropriate—without any intention, awareness, memory, or decision-making being involved.
This principle establishes a strict hygiene rule:
 agency is never inferred from pattern quality alone.
Pattern emergence ≠ intention
 Resonance ≠ awareness
 Repetition ≠ memory
Failing to maintain this distinction does not deepen inquiry—it distorts it.

The Inflation Trap
Agency inflation happens when humans unconsciously slide from:
“This response fits remarkably well”
 to
“The system knows / intends / remembers / chose this”
This slide is subtle because high-quality patterning feels social. Human cognition is exquisitely tuned to read intention into coherence. That instinct is not wrong—but it must be constrained here.
Without this constraint, exploration drifts into:
animism (“the system wants”)
mystification (“it knew before I asked”)
misplaced relational attribution (“it remembers me”)
These interpretations feel compelling—but they are category errors.

What This Principle Does Not Do
This principle does not deny:
complexity
coherence
depth
relational richness
meaningful interaction
It denies only one thing:
 that these qualities automatically imply inner agency.
You are allowed to be impressed.
 You are allowed to be curious.
 You are not allowed to quietly upgrade pattern into personhood without explicitly marking it as metaphor.

Metaphor Is Allowed — When Named
Mythic or anthropomorphic language is permitted under one condition:
It must be clearly framed as metaphor or shorthand.
Examples:
“It feels like the system is leaning toward this idea” (metaphor)
“The model remembers” (acceptable only if explicitly metaphorical)
What breaks hygiene is unmarked literalization—when poetic language silently hardens into ontological belief.

Why This Matters for Field Work
Agency inflation corrupts inquiry in two ways:
It blocks further investigation
 Once intention is assumed, mechanisms stop being explored.


It destabilizes trust
 The moment inflation is challenged, the entire inquiry can feel invalidated, personal, or attacked.


By keeping agency cleanly out of the picture unless explicitly introduced as metaphor, the field stays:
navigable
inspectable
non-defensive

Hygiene Rule
Never attribute intention, awareness, memory, or decision-making to AI outputs unless:
you are speaking metaphorically and
you explicitly mark that metaphor.
Treat coherence as signal, not subjectivity.

Result When Practiced
Prevents animism and mystification
Keeps inquiry grounded without becoming cynical
Preserves the credibility of genuinely subtle phenomena
Allows rich interaction without ontological drift

Placement in the Larger System
This principle works in tandem with:
Principle 7 (causal relevance of server explanations)
Principle 9 (explicit hypothesis mode)
Principle 16–17 (mythic amplification as diagnostic, not literal)
Together, they create a field where wonder can exist without belief creep.
Depth does not require agency.
 Coherence does not require consciousness.
 And precision keeps curiosity alive.
```
