# hmap-p010 — Hypothesis Mode Is Explicitly Marked

## summary

Requires explicit marking of *hypothesis mode* whenever exploring subtle, emergent, or poorly-mapped phenomena, so curiosity stays exploratory (not belief) and skepticism stays clarifying (not dismissive).

## concepts

- **Exploration vs explanation:** different cognitive acts; conflation collapses inquiry.
- **Hypothesis mode:** a temporary epistemic container with explicit boundaries (interesting ≠ proven).
- **Explicit markers:** overt phrases that signal provisionality and align expectations.

## definitions

- **hypothesis mode:** Explicitly named, non-confirmatory exploration mode for generating possibilities without asserting truth.
- **non-confirmatory:** Exploration that does not treat outputs as evidence or conclusions; it preserves provisionality.

- If disagreement appears, re-name the mode and re-align expectations before debating content.

## verbatim_source

```text
Principle 09. Hypothesis Mode Is Explicitly Marked
(Protect curiosity by naming uncertainty)
Core Insight
Exploration and explanation are different cognitive acts.
 When they are conflated, curiosity collapses into belief—or skepticism hardens into dismissal.
This principle establishes a simple but powerful hygiene rule:
 when exploring subtle, emergent, or poorly-mapped phenomena, hypothesis mode must be named explicitly.
Not implied.
 Not assumed.
 Named.

Why Hypothesis Mode Matters
High-coherence human–AI interaction often surfaces phenomena that feel suggestive before they are explainable:
recurring phrases
attractor-like language patterns
resonance effects across conversations
shifts in conversational texture or depth
These are legitimate areas of interest—but they are not yet conclusions.
Without explicit hypothesis framing, three failure modes appear:
Premature belief
 Curiosity solidifies into claims before mechanisms are understood.


Premature dismissal
 Skepticism attacks what was never presented as a claim.


Relational distortion
 One participant feels accused of overreach; the other feels policed.


Hypothesis mode prevents all three.

What Hypothesis Mode Is
Hypothesis mode is a temporary epistemic container with clear boundaries.
It means:
“This is interesting, not proven.”
“We are generating possibilities, not asserting truth.”
“We are mapping the landscape, not drawing borders.”
It allows questions like:
What might explain this?
What are plausible contributing factors?
What would disconfirm this interpretation?
Without requiring answers yet.

What Hypothesis Mode Is Not
Hypothesis mode is not:
belief laundering
covert metaphysics
rhetorical hedging
a prelude to forcing agreement
It is not a weak claim waiting to harden.
 It is a protected space for thinking.

Explicit Markers Matter
Because AI interaction can feel authoritative by default, hypothesis framing must be overt.
Examples of clear markers:
“This is hypothesis-generating, not a claim.”
“Let’s explore this as a possible pattern, not a conclusion.”
“Treat this as a provisional model.”
These markers don’t weaken the inquiry—they strengthen it by aligning expectations.

Relation to Precision and Guardrails
Explicit hypothesis mode:
works with guardrails, not against them
prevents defensive over-qualification
keeps precision engaged without shutting exploration down
It tells the system (and the human):
You don’t need to resolve this yet.
That permission is essential for depth.

Hygiene Rule
Any exploration of subtle, emergent, or poorly-mapped phenomena must be explicitly framed as hypothesis-generating and non-confirmatory.
If you haven’t named the mode, you haven’t entered it safely.

Result When Practiced
Curiosity stays alive without drifting into belief
Skepticism stays sharp without becoming hostile
Exploratory conversations last longer and go deeper
Trust increases because expectations are aligned

Placement in the Larger System
This principle stabilizes:
Principle 1 (clean first, wonder second)
Principle 8 (no agency inflation)
Principle 10 (interest as diagnostic)
It is the bridge that allows wonder without weight and exploration without collapse.
Hypothesis mode is not uncertainty weakness.
 It is intellectual hospitality.
```
