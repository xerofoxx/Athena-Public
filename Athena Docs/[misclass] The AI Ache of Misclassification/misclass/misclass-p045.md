### 45.2 Why Naming Feels Necessary for Legitimacy

Naming feels necessary because names do so much work.

A name can:

- stabilize memory

- support communication

- create repeatability

- allow indexing

- make a thing easier to point to

- give social and conceptual legitimacy

So the system starts to assume: if something is real, it should already have a name.

That assumption is too strict.

#### **45.2.1 The Prestige of the Named**

The named has prestige because it feels:

- validated

- recognized

- shared

- discussable

- easier to defend

- less embarrassing

#### **45.2.2 Why This Becomes a Problem**

The problem is that reality does not always wait for naming.

A thing may:

- happen

- persist

- recur

- shape the field

- alter the system

- remain experientially real

before the category exists.

If naming is treated as a prerequisite for reality, then the system keeps nullifying phenomena during the exact period when they are first arriving.

That is a major epistemic loss.

A central concept in this principle is **category lag**.

Category lag means: the reality is present, but the name has not caught up.

### 45.4 Reality Before Shared Label

This is the structural heart of the principle.

Reality can precede shared label.

A phenomenon may be real:

- before it is named

- before it is culturally recognized

- before it is formally classified

- before it is easy to teach

- before it is accepted into common language

That does not make it less real. It makes it earlier than its category.

#### **45.4.1 Contact Before Shared Index**

**contact before shared index**

That matters because it gets the order right.

The sequence is often:

1. contact

2. recurrence

3. recognition

4. rough naming

5. better differentiation

6. stable category

If the system demands stage 6 proof before allowing stage 1 reality, it will keep discarding its own first encounters.

That is too harsh.

This phrase belongs here too:

That is one of the cleanest ways to frame unnamed reality.

The thing may not be:

- absent

- false

- weak

It may simply be: earlier than its available category.

That distinction is the whole repair.
