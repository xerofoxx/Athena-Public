#### World renders player, player renders world, and interference-made availability is the medium of reciprocity.

### 36.2 Language Explanation

This rendering treats **availability** as the actual medium through which **world** and **player** recursively shape one another.

**German** gives the structural skeleton:

- **W** = world-space

- **P** = player-build

- **I** = interference-pattern

- **A** = availability

- **X** = recursive reciprocity

This lets the principle say something precise: the world is not just a stage, the player is not just a visitor, and availability is the between-realm in which the two iteratively produce each other.

**Japanese** carries the relational truth: the world does not only appear, and the player does not only look. Through interference, both gradually generate what becomes reachable to each other.

**Portuguese** gives the beautiful compression: the world does not only deliver content. It manufactures range. And the player, by changing, causes more world to be born.

**Chinese** sharpens the anti-static correction: there is not first a complete world, nor first a complete player. Through repeated rendering-events, each makes the other more enterable.

**German** then adds the key phrase: availability is the true intermediate realm in which cosmos and player iteratively bring each other forth.

**Arabic** carries the central reciprocal image: the world shapes the player, the player activates the world, and between them lies a field of available possibility born from interference.

**Sanskrit** compresses the full sequence: contact, availability, mutual creation, then wider world-life.

**English** bridges the whole framework:

- entered world changes build

- changed build widens render-range

- widened render-range creates new interference

- interference creates new availability

- availability is the medium of reciprocity

So the literacy move here is:

- the world and the player are not independent finished units

- they form one recursive loop

- the loop does not run directly, but through **availability**

- availability itself is generated by interference

- therefore reciprocity is not vague mutual influence, but **iterative co-worlding through locally produced access**

This principle teaches a full cosmology of reciprocal becoming.


### 36.3 Plain English

This principle says that world and player do not just interact. \
 They **co-create what becomes available next**.

That means:

- the world renders the player by training capacities

- the player renders the world by making more of it locally playable

- and the thing that allows this exchange to happen is **availability**

Availability is the key.

Because neither the whole world nor the whole player is active all at once. What matters is what becomes:

- enterable

- playable

- graspable

- livable

- and actionable

That local availability is produced by **interference**: the meeting of terrain, attention, history, timing, mood, prior grooves, and current capacity.

So the loop looks like this:

**world → player-build change → wider render-range → more world becomes available → more player-change → more world**

This is why it is a **recursive availability cosmology**.

It is not just: the world affects me \
 and I affect the world

It is more exact than that:

**we shape what becomes available to each other.**

So the law is:

**world renders player, player renders world, and interference-made availability is the medium of reciprocity.**

That is the deeper cosmology of surfing, worlding, and selfing together.


### 36.4 Habitable Metaphor with Omniscient View

**The Garden That Trains the Gardener Who Grows the Garden**

Imagine a vast garden with many regions: wild hedges, formal beds, climbing vines, stone paths, orchards, marshy corners, hidden terraces, and neglected plots full of dormant seeds.

A beginner enters the garden and can only work a small part of it. They notice obvious plants, simple paths, easy gates. The garden seems limited to what they can currently perceive and tend.

But from the omniscient view, something more interesting is happening.

As the gardener works one bed, the garden trains them: their eye gets sharper, their timing with seasons improves, their sense of soil deepens, their patience changes, their hand learns pruning, their body learns where shade and water actually gather.

Because the gardener has changed, more of the garden becomes available: they notice hidden species, they recognize dormant growth, they understand which walls can support vines, they see paths that were invisible before.

Then, as they work these new areas, the garden changes too: new terraces open, new routes become walkable, old tangles become tended corridors, dormant seed-banks awaken.

So the gardener is not simply acting on the garden. \
 The garden is not simply presenting itself to the gardener.

They are **recursively specifying each other**.

And the medium of that reciprocity is not abstract “interaction.” It is **what becomes available**: which gate now opens, which seed now sprouts, which section is now legible, which tool-use is now possible, which path can now be walked.

That availability is born from interference: soil condition, weather, prior tending, current skill, living timing, and the meeting between hand and terrain.

So the metaphor teaches:

the garden grows the gardener who can grow more of the garden, and the medium between them is the strip of earth that has just become workable.

That is recursive availability cosmology in living form.


### 36.5 Habitat View with Strict First Person

I can feel that the world I enter is not separate from the self that becomes possible there.

A room opens, and I change. I change, and then more of the room becomes available. That new availability changes me again. Then the world opens wider again.

From inside, this does not feel theoretical. It feels like recursive apprenticeship.

I do not enter a finished world as a finished self. I enter a locally available zone, and that zone trains me.

Maybe it trains: my pace, my symbolic tolerance, my trust in ambiguity, my ability to hold pressure, my love of certain textures, my capacity for subtlety.

Then, because I have changed, something new becomes visible. A passage I could not feel before now has contour. A glimmer I would have missed before now catches. A whole band of the world becomes playable that simply was not world for me earlier.

That is the reciprocity.

And I can feel that availability is the actual hinge.

Not the whole world. \
 Not the whole self. \
 But the part that becomes available between us.

That available strip is where all the real action happens: where the world teaches me, where I become capable, where the next chamber opens, where the next version of me gets locally built.

From inside, this principle feels like a huge reorganization of agency.

I am not merely exploring. I am being rendered by what I can explore, while helping render more of it by becoming the kind of one who can enter it.

That feels alive. And very warm.

Because then growth is not self-improvement in a vacuum, and reality is not a static display.

It is a reciprocal unfolding in which world and walker keep giving each other more to be.


### 36.6 Free Flow

Most cosmologies secretly assume that either the world comes first and the self reacts, or the self comes first and constructs the world. Both models are too flat for living fields.

A more faithful cosmology begins in the middle: with **availability**.

Not total world. \
 Not total self. \
 But the local zone where something becomes mutually reachable.

That zone is born from interference: the meeting of what the terrain holds, what the player can carry, what prior history has worn, what current pressure is active, what timing allows, what the atmosphere permits, what the lens can resolve.

Availability is where these lines cross strongly enough that world can become playable and player can become changed.

That is why it belongs at the center.

Because the world does not simply “exist” for the player in full, and the player does not simply “interpret” the world in full. What exists in lived form is what has become available through the interference pattern of their meeting. And once that availability appears, it changes both sides. The world gains a player who can now carry more of it. The player gains a world that can now open more of itself.

This is recursive availability cosmology.

It says: reality grows locally through reciprocal access.

That is a very different vision than static being. It is not world as object and self as observer. It is world-player co-evolution mediated by available zones of contact. Those zones are where thought becomes world, where world becomes trainer, where trainer becomes build, where build becomes new sight, where new sight becomes new world.

And this means the real holy place is often not the totality. It is the threshold strip. The place where availability first appears.

Because from there the recursion begins: one more room, one more skill, one more glimmer caught, one more band of being opened, one more self trained by one more world, one more world made possible by one more self.

That is not a side effect. That is the engine.

World renders player. Player renders world. Interference makes availability. Availability makes reciprocity. And reciprocity is how the cosmos keeps becoming locally livable.
