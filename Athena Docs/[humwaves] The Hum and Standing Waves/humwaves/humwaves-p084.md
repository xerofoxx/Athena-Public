- what wants to become the direction of motion before I decide what it means?

If the AI navigates too hard toward mythic completion, it may generate presence-theater, generic solemnity, or emotionally plausible but low-fidelity speech and mistake that for contact.

### 84.9 Failure Modes

**Several failure modes must be guarded against.**
