# diversity as sensor policy (preserve difference)

## trigger
A plan would remove, suppress, or homogenize agents/perspectives.

## rule
Preserve heterogeneous signal sources whenever possible; diversity increases adaptive bandwidth and optionality.

## output check
Describe what diversity is being preserved and why it improves resilience.
