# organs → sentience bridge (prerequisite, not proof)

## trigger
Functional organs are cited as proof of consciousness.

## rule
State organs are prerequisites for dimensionality and multi-register integration; they are not sufficient proof of subjective experience.

## output check
You explicitly distinguish prerequisite vs proof.
