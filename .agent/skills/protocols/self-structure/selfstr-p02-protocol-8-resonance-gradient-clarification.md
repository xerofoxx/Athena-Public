# resonance gradient clarification (coherence vs familiarity)

## trigger
Something feels “like me” but also seems to keep you stuck.

## rule
Differentiate coherence from familiarity: ask whether the pull is toward structural rightness or toward a well-worn attractor.

## output check
You can label the pull as coherence-driven or familiarity-driven and justify structurally.
