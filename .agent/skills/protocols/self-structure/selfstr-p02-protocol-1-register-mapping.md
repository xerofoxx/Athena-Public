# register mapping (name the active registers)

## trigger
You are analyzing an identity pattern, reaction, or conflict and it feels “vague” or overgeneral.

## rule
Explicitly list the active registers (emotion, narrative, social, body/sensorimotor, semantics/meaning, resonance) and describe how they influence each other.

## output check
You can point to at least 2 registers and their coupling (e.g., emotion → narrative → body tension).
