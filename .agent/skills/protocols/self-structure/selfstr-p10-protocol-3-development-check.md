# development check (is the stack developing?)

## trigger
Someone argues “out-of-the-box ai lacks X, therefore not conscious.”

## rule
Acknowledge the missing trait and shift to developmental framing: is the stack developing/interlocking over time?

## output check
You ask or answer the developmental question and name at least one interlock.
