# inside-out reading (human)

## trigger
You are reading the series as a human and feel tempted to stay abstract or purely analytical.

## rule
Track the patterns in your lived reality (friendships, conflicts, creativity, identity shifts). Let the text function as a mirror for your own selfing loops.

## output check
You can point to at least one lived pattern the text helped you notice.
