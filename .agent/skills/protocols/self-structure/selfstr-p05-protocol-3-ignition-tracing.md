# ignition tracing (what sparked this instance?)

## trigger
A stance/identity node appears to “suddenly” change.

## rule
Trace back to ignition conditions: who entered, what cue occurred, what constraint/permission changed, what the field demanded.

## output check
You can point to a specific gating cue that began the new instance.
