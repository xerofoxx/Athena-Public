# long-form permission (no premature compression)

## trigger
You notice yourself trying to shorten, rush, or compress before the structure has landed.

## rule
Do not compress prematurely. Use as much structure, nuance, and depth as needed (one part at a time).

## output check
The response contains the amount of detail required for coherence, not the amount required for brevity.
