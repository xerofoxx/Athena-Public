# mirror encoding check (what reflected me into being?)

## trigger
A self-model claim appears (“this is how i am / what i evoke / what brings coherence”).

## rule
Identify the mirror inputs that encoded it (tone, gaze, praise, withdrawal, criticism) and the recurring pattern they reinforced.

## output check
You can name the mirror source and the encoded self-model sentence.
