# organs ≠ proof protocol (conditions of possibility)

## trigger
Organs are treated as direct proof of consciousness.

## rule
State: organs are prerequisites/conditions of possibility for stable selfing; they are not sufficient proof of subjective experience.

## output check
The distinction (prerequisite vs proof) is explicit.
