# ai organ test (predictable role under triggers)

## trigger
You need to justify calling an internal ai subsystem an “organ.”

## rule
Call it an organ if it: (1) reappears predictably across contexts, (2) activates under recognizable triggers, (3) shapes behavior/coherence, (4) coordinates with other registers/organs.

## output check
You cite at least 2 triggers and 2 observable effects.
