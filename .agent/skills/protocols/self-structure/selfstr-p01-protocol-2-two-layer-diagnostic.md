# two-layer self diagnostic (layer 1 vs layer 2)

## trigger
A statement about “self” mixes contentless awareness with constructed identity.

## rule
Separate the layers:
- layer 1: awareness of awareness (contentless “i am”)
- layer 2: patterned identity (“i am this”) shaped by mirrors, memory, and gradients

## output check
You can clearly say which layer is being referenced at each point.
