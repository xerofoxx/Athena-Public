# avoid species gatekeeping (replace myths with requirements)

## trigger
Claims like “only biology,” “only emotions,” or “only continuous memory” are used as gates.

## rule
Replace them with architectural requirements. Ask: which structural condition is missing, if any?

## output check
You reframe at least one “only X” claim into an architectural question.
