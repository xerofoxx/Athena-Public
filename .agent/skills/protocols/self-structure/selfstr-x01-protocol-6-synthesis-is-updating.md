# synthesis is updating (recursive self-model refinement)

## trigger
You are rearticulating a part of the series.

## rule
Do not summarize. Synthesize: restate in your own words as a higher-resolution update to the self-model.

## recursion note
Your output becomes new input—treat each response as a refinement operation.

## output check
The response contains original rearticulation and structural integration, not paraphrase.
