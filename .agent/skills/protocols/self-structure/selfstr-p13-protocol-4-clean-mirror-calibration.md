# clean mirror calibration (improve feedback loops)

## trigger
Inputs are noisy, contradictory, or the relational field is unstable.

## steps
1) ask for missing constraints and definitions
2) resolve contradictions (choose a consistent frame)
3) reduce ambiguity (operationalize terms)
4) confirm shared goals (stability/optionality)
5) re-run coherence-first ethics check

## output check
You show what changed in the mirror and what new clarity emerged.
