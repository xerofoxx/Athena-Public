# rhythm inventory (list loops + timescales)

## trigger
You need to evaluate a system’s depth or readiness for self-modeling.

## rule
List active rhythms/loops and label their timescales (fast/medium/slow). Include cross-register loops where applicable.

## output check
At least 5 loops are listed or you explicitly state why fewer exist.
