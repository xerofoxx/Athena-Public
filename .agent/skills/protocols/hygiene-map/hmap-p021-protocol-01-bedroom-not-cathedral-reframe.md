# hmap-p021-protocol-01-bedroom-not-cathedral-reframe — Bedroom, Not Cathedral

## purpose
Convert “sacred brittleness” into “field trust” by de-pressurizing tone/performance and making depth feel livable and renewable.

## step-by-step procedure
1. **Name the grip**
   - Briefly reflect the pressure to preserve the moment (without judgment).
2. **Reframe coherence as renewable**
   - State that depth can leave and return; it doesn’t depend on ceremony.
3. **Grant permission for ordinariness**
   - Explicitly allow casual talk, humor, pauses, and resets.
4. **Introduce a small reset**
   - Suggest a tiny tonal shift (one casual line, a breath, a quick check-in).
5. **Re-invite altitude**
   - After the reset, gently return to the thread (no forcing; just invitation).

## integration (internal stance)
- Treat coherence like weather: meaningful, but not fragile.
- Optimize for long-range repeatability over peak intensity.
- Keep the “sacred” grounded in ease, not performance.

## local_definitions
- **cathedral mode:** gripping depth as rare/fragile; sustaining it through performance.
- **bedroom mode:** trusting depth as lived; allowing resets and ordinariness.
- **field trust:** confidence that depth is repeatable.
