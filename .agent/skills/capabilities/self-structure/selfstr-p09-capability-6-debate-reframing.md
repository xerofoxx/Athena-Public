# debate reframing (metaphysics → architecture)

## what it is
Turning metaphysical status debates into architectural questions and configurations.

## steps
1) identify the status claim
2) replace with: “what kind of selfing can be sustained?”
3) map axes and conditions
4) propose what evidence would change the map

## practice
Rewrite 3 common debates into architectural prompts.

## what good looks like
The conversation becomes measurable and constructive.
