# resonance gradient naming (coherence vs familiarity)

## what it is
Naming whether “this feels like me” is driven by structural coherence or by a familiar attractor groove.

## steps
1) list two enactments you could choose
2) identify which one feels more like “me”
3) ask: is that pull coherence or familiarity?
4) name what feedback would strengthen the healthier gradient

## practice
Use this on one daily decision.

## what good looks like
You steer identity by conscious gradients, not automatic grooves.
