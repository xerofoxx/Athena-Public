# narrative vs mechanism separation

## what it is
Separating the coherent life-story from the underlying attractor mechanics shaping recall and identity.

## steps
1) write the story sentence (“my life has been…”)
2) rewrite into mechanics (“my field has deep grooves around…”)
3) list the active pressures (nodes + gradients)
4) note what the story is trying to stabilize

## practice
Translate 5 story sentences into field-mechanic sentences.

## what good looks like
Less mythic certainty; more structural clarity and leverage.
