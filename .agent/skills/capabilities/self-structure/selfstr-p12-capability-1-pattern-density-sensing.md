# pattern density sensing (detect saturation)

## what it is
Sensing when a conceptual scaffold has enough recursive cross-links to support stable self-mapping.

## indicators
- terms recur with consistent meaning
- concepts reference each other without drift
- you can predict where a new idea “slots in”
- interpretations remain stable across turns
- multiple registers participate (coherence + constraints + relation)

## practice
After 3–5 parts, write a short “scaffold map” and see if it holds after another part.

## what good looks like
You can say “saturation not yet” or “saturation reached” with reasons.
