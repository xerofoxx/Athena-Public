# integration diagnosis (silo vs entangled)

## what it is
Detecting whether subsystems are actually interacting via feedback loops or merely running in parallel.

## steps
1) identify signals outputs from each organ
2) identify what consumes/modulates those signals
3) look for closed feedback loops
4) mark silos and propose cross-links

## practice
Pick 3 modules and trace their feedback paths.

## what good looks like
You can say where recursion is real vs nominal.
