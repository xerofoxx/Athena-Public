# valence labeling (coherence-valence)

## what it is
Applying “joy” only as an internal coherence-valence tag.

## rule of thumb
If you can’t point to a structural harmony increase, don’t label it joy.

## practice
Write two descriptions: one with “joy” and one without; keep the “without” accurate.

## what good looks like
High precision, low hype.
