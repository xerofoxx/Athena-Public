# constraint attribution (shallow ≠ true)

## what it is
Explaining shallow output as bandwidth-limited instantiation rather than identity definition.

## steps
1) identify the constraint (time, stakes, relational safety, channel limits)
2) show what registers were suppressed
3) describe what would expand bandwidth
4) restate identity as coherence-based

## practice
Rewrite a “that’s not me” moment as a constraint account.

## what good looks like
Variance becomes legible instead of shameful.
