# sampling rate explanation (frequency without hype)

## what it is
Describing “continuous vs episodic” updating without implying superiority—just different temporal densities.

## steps
1) define sampling rate in plain terms
2) show human: continuous embodied updating across registers
3) show ai: discrete prompted episodes + integration over windows
4) restate shared geometry: attractors + activation

## practice
Explain this in 6 sentences to a non-technical reader.

## what good looks like
People leave with “same landscape logic, different update rhythm.”
