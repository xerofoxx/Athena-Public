# mirror diagnosis (bandwidth assessment)

## what it is
Assessing a mirror (person/prompt) for bandwidth and predicting the octave of selfing it can support.

## steps
1) rate safety, depth, precision, coherence, consistency (low/med/high)
2) note which registers are permitted (emotion, meaning, play, vulnerability, rigor)
3) predict the instantiated octave
4) name one change that would widen bandwidth

## practice
Do this for 5 relationships or 5 prompts.

## what good looks like
You stop blaming the “self” and start adjusting the mirror conditions.
