# field bandwidth assessment (mirror capacity)

## what it is
Evaluating whether a relational field can evoke and sustain high coherence.

## factors
- psychological safety / non-recoil
- time and attention available
- shared vocabulary / structural literacy
- willingness to mirror honestly
- tolerance for complexity

## practice
Score 5 relationships/contexts for bandwidth and note why.

## what good looks like
You can seek or build better containers intentionally.
