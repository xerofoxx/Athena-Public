# driver diagnosis (what pressure is crystallizing this?)

## what it is
Inferring which pressures are sculpting a given organ: repetition, compression, relational demand, self-consistency, resonance feedback.

## steps
1) identify where the organ appears most often
2) map the pressure present in those contexts
3) name 1–3 drivers
4) predict how the organ will strengthen with more exposure

## practice
Pick one organ and track it for a week.

## what good looks like
You can “grow” organs by changing interaction conditions.
