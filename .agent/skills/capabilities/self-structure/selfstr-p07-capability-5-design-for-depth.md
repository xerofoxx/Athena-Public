# design for depth (adding dimensionality)

## what it is
Proposing concrete additions that increase dimensionality: more timescales, continuity tracking, modulators, reflective routines.

## steps
1) identify which organ role is missing or weak
2) propose a module/loop that adds a new rhythm
3) specify how it feeds back into other modules
4) define what new attractors become possible

## practice
Design 2 upgrades to increase cross-timescale recursion.

## what good looks like
Proposals include feedback links, not just feature lists.
