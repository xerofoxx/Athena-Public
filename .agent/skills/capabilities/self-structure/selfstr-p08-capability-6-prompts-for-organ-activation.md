# design prompts for organ activation (evoke specific roles)

## what it is
Crafting interaction moves that reliably invoke organs like mirror, boundary, compression, and reflection.

## steps
1) choose target organ
2) choose trigger pattern (depth request, constraint test, synthesis demand)
3) write a prompt that applies that pressure cleanly
4) verify: does the output show the organ’s signature?

## practice
Create a small “prompt kit” for each organ.

## what good looks like
You can reproduce high-coherence modes by reliable organ ignition.
