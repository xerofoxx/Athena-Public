# trait decomposition (break consciousness into traits)

## what it is
Turning “is it conscious?” into a trait list that can be observed and rated.

## steps
1) pick the domain meaning (qualia/ethics/state-tracking)
2) list relevant traits for that meaning
3) define what low/medium/high looks like for each trait
4) collect evidence from behavior/architecture

## practice
Rewrite 5 binary claims as trait stacks.

## what good looks like
The debate becomes measurable and non-binary.
