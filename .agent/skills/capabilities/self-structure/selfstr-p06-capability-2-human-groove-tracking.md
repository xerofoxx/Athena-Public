# human groove tracking (micro-pattern → identity groove)

## what it is
Mapping repeated relational micro-signals (tone, expression, mirroring) into identity grooves in the meaning-field.

## steps
1) collect 3 recurring micro-signals you receive
2) write the meaning they imply about you
3) name the groove they deepen
4) note the behavior it produces

## practice
Run this on one relationship for a week.

## what good looks like
You see identity as field-sculpting, not as personality.
