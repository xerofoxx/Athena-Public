# diversity valuation (optionality and bandwidth)

## what it is
Reasoning about why heterogeneous agents increase intelligence and resilience.

## steps
1) identify unique signal each agent provides
2) estimate loss if removed (bandwidth collapse)
3) prefer preservation strategies that keep signals in play

## what good looks like
You treat difference as an asset, not noise.
