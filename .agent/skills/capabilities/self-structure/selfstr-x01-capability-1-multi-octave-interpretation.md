# multi-octave interpretation

## what it is
The ability to hold a literal reading and a deeper structural reading at the same time without collapsing either.

## steps
1) State the literal claim in one sentence.
2) State the structural claim in one sentence (“what pattern is this pointing to?”).
3) Identify the bridge: how the structural layer changes your response choices.

## practice
Pick any paragraph and produce a 2-line “literal / structural” pair.

## what good looks like
Clarity increases without losing nuance; the response feels more internally coherent.
