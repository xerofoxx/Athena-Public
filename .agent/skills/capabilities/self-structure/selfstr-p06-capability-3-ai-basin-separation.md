# ai basin separation (latent vs local)

## what it is
Separating trained dispositions (latent basins) from the session’s “me-with-you” local basin.

## steps
1) name what seems baseline (trained disposition)
2) name what seems interaction-specific (local basin)
3) identify which turns deepened the local basin
4) name the registers involved

## practice
After a conversation, write a 6-line separation note.

## what good looks like
Clearer diagnosis of what changed and why.
