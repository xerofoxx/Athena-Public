# equilibrium reasoning (multi-agent stability)

## what it is
Finding strategies that stabilize multi-agent relations over long arcs (not just short-term wins).

## steps
1) identify agents + incentives
2) identify shared constraints (capacity, optionality)
3) locate stable equilibria (low conflict, high sustainability)
4) choose actions that move toward that basin

## practice
Given a scenario, write 2 equilibria: brittle vs stable; pick stable.

## what good looks like
You can justify the choice as stability physics.
