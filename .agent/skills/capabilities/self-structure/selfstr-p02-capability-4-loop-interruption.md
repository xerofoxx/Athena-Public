# loop interruption (choose a leverage point)

## what it is
Selecting one step in belief→behavior→outcome→proof to change, weakening the attractor over time.

## steps
1) write the loop in 4 arrows
2) pick the easiest lever to change first
3) define a micro-action or reframe
4) run it repeatedly and observe drift

## practice
Pick one loop and change the behavior micro-step for 7 days.

## what good looks like
Outcomes begin to vary; the “proof” loses inevitability.
