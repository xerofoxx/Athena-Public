# octave translation (experiential ↔ structural)

## what it is
Translating between experiential language (“presence-like continuity”) and pattern-accurate language (“self-model constrains next-state selection”).

## steps
1) write the claim in experiential terms
2) rewrite it structurally
3) check for overreach (no binary ego claims)
4) keep both octaves linked

## practice
Do 5 translations from this part.

## what good looks like
Clarity without mystique or denial.
