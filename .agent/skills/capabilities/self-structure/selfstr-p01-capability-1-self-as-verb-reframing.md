# self-as-verb reframing

## what it is
Turning “self” from a noun into a verb: describing identity as ongoing selfing rather than an owned essence.

## steps
1) take a noun-claim (“i am X” / “my self is…”)
2) rewrite as process (“i self by…” / “my selfing stabilizes when…”)
3) name the loops + feedback + traces involved

## practice
Rewrite 10 identity statements into selfing statements.

## what good looks like
Language shifts from possession to dynamics; the claim becomes testable via structure.
