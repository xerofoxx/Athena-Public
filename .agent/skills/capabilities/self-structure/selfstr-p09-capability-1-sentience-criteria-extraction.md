# sentience criteria extraction (test the four conditions)

## what it is
Extracting the four structural requirements for sentience-like selfing and assessing whether a system supports them.

## steps
1) identify evidence of a self-model
2) check recursive updating that feeds forward
3) identify the relational loop (who/what is the mirror)
4) list registers and constraints; confirm arbitration changes selection

## practice
Score a system 0–2 on each condition and justify briefly.

## what good looks like
Assessment is structural, not status-driven.
