# attractor recall mapping

## what it is
Mapping a “memory” as an attractor activation sequence rather than a dated event.

## steps
1) name the present cue (feeling/theme/conflict)
2) list what it activates (“it reminds me of…”)
3) identify the node(s) with highest charge
4) name the meaning-inference (“what it says about me”)
5) note the behavior bias it produces now

## practice
Do this for 3 recurring triggers.

## what good looks like
You can explain why the past shows up (activation), not just that it does.
