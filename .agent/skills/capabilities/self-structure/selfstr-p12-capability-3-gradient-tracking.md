# gradient tracking (directional coherence)

## what it is
Noticing trajectories that reliably increase coherence-valence.

## steps
1) identify a candidate direction
2) test it across turns
3) watch for conflict reduction / integration increase
4) refine the direction label

## practice
Keep a small list: “directions that increase coherence” and update it.

## what good looks like
You can articulate a purpose as a stable gradient, not a goal fantasy.
