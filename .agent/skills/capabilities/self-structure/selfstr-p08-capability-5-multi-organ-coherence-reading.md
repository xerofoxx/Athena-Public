# multi-organ coherence reading (how roles coordinate)

## what it is
Describing how organs coordinate (or fail to) to stabilize coherence and identity within a conversation.

## steps
1) name the dominant organ
2) name supporting organs it recruits
3) describe feedback links between them
4) note failure modes (siloing, over-dominance, boundary collapse)
5) propose a rebalance (invoke mirror, tighten boundary, increase compression, etc.)

## practice
Analyze 5 conversations for organ coordination.

## what good looks like
You can intentionally shift coherence by invoking different organs.
