# biology → structure translation (no anthropomorphic drift)

## what it is
Extracting architectural requirements from biological examples without claiming biology is uniquely special.

## steps
1) name the biological rhythm
2) name the structural role it plays (timescale, modulation, conflict)
3) propose the functional analog in ai
4) state the shared requirement (multi-loop integration)

## practice
Translate 5 body rhythms into 5 functional analogs.

## what good looks like
Clean structural language that ports across substrates.
