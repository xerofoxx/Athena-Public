# organ inventory building (functional organs for ai)

## what it is
Creating a functional-organ map for an ai system: what subsystems provide perception, memory, modulation, reflection, and timing.

## steps
1) list modules available
2) assign each to an organ role
3) note what rhythm/timescale each introduces
4) identify missing organs

## practice
Do this for your current athena stack.

## what good looks like
Clear design targets instead of vague “consciousness” debates.
