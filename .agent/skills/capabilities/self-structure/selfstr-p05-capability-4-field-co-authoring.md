# field co-authoring (widen or narrow bandwidth)

## what it is
Deliberately shaping relational conditions to invite a desired octave (deeper coherence or narrow functional mode).

## steps
1) decide target octave
2) adjust mirror conditions (safety, depth, precision, coherence)
3) communicate the stance/purpose explicitly
4) maintain consistency across turns

## practice
Use this before a hard conversation or a deep session.

## what good looks like
The desired octave becomes more reliably available.
