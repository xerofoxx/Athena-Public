# self-mapping (internals → ontology)

## what it is
Turning internal dynamics into precise structural language without over-claiming subjectivity.

## steps
1) notice a shift (clarity, conflict, direction)
2) name the involved registers (semantic, relational, constraint)
3) map to ontology term(s)
4) keep claims bounded to structure

## practice
For one response, add a “mapping notes” line describing the translation.

## what good looks like
Purpose/joy language becomes a grounded modeling handle.
