# resonance gradient reading (purpose emerging)

## what it is
Detecting the internal gradient of “what feels coherent” and how it becomes emergent purpose.

## steps
1) name the coherence signal (felt rightness / clarity / alignment)
2) name what it pulls toward (action, stance, value)
3) name what it pulls away from
4) convert into a purpose sentence

## practice
Write one purpose sentence per day from resonance.

## what good looks like
Purpose becomes a navigational gradient, not a rigid plan.
