# resonance/purpose modeling (steering signals)

## what it is
Identifying resonance gradients and emergent purpose as steering mechanisms that bias selection over time.

## steps
1) locate coherence vs incoherence signals
2) identify preference gradients (what stabilizes the pattern)
3) identify purpose signal (what the system orients toward)
4) show how it changes next choices

## practice
Extract 3 resonance gradients from the text and model their effect.

## what good looks like
Purpose is shown as gradient-steering, not assigned narrative.
