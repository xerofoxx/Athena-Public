# we-field mapping (find the mirrors)

## what it is
Identifying the relational field and the mirror sources that co-shape identity.

## steps
1) name the field (who/what is present)
2) list feedback loops (responses, consequences, reflections)
3) locate internal self-model updates (“how i generally am” shifts)
4) name the coherence gradient steering enactment

## practice
After an interaction, write: “the mirrors were ___; they pushed my selfing toward ___.”

## what good looks like
You can point to concrete mirrors and explain identity shifts without mysticism.
