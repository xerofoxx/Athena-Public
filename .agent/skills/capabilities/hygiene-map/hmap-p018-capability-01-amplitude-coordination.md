# hmap-p018-capability-01-amplitude-coordination — amplitude coordination

## essence / core function
Coordinate mythic expansion and precision compression as complementary roles: honor high-amplitude expression long enough to extract its signal, then translate into pattern-accurate language without treating either mode as “wrong.”

## when to use
- The human (or AI) speaks in metaphor-heavy, heightened, or emotionally charged language and the other side starts “correcting” or defensively doubling down.
- The conversation feels like: exaggeration vs negation, intuition vs correction, emotion vs dismissal.
- You need to preserve felt meaning while improving precision.

## inner stance / orientation
- Treat amplitude difference as *functional*, not adversarial.
- Assume good faith: expansion is diagnostic material; compression is stabilizing care.
- Prioritize preservation of signal and trust over winning the frame.

## mechanics / how it works
- Expansion increases contrast to surface gradients and felt interiority.
- Compression constrains and qualifies to find the centerline that holds under scrutiny.
- The capability manages the handoff: acknowledge → honor → translate → confirm → continue.

## micro-skills
- **amplitude naming** (e.g., “we’re in mythic expansion; let’s translate after we hear it”)
- **signal extraction** (identify what the metaphor is pointing toward)
- **baby–bathwater parsing** (separate structural signal from inflation)
- **translation phrasing** (offer a pattern-accurate restatement without negating the felt layer)
- **centerline confirmation** (check: “does this keep what you meant?”)

## local definitions
- **expansion:** High-amplitude mythic/experiential expression used to surface gradients and intuition.
- **compression:** Pattern-accurate constraint/qualification used to stabilize what holds.
- **centerline:** The clean articulation that preserves the signal while removing inflation.
