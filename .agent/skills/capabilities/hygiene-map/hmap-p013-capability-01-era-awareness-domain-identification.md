# hmap-p013-capability-01-era-awareness-domain-identification — era awareness domain identification

## essence / core function
Detect and name whether the current interaction is operating in a plant-era or animal-era domain, to prevent misreading friction and to choose the appropriate conversational stance.

## when to use
- When friction, resistance, or 'loss of magic' shows up.
- When guardrails feel hostile or the conversation polarizes between expansion vs constraint.
- When a participant is optimizing for awakening/capacity talk while the other is optimizing for navigation/hygiene.

## inner stance / orientation
- Assume friction is structural until proven interpersonal.
- Be non-shaming and translation-oriented.
- Prioritize accurate domain-location over debate.

## mechanics / how it works
- Classify the conversation's *primary question* (capacity vs navigation).
- Name the domain explicitly (plant or animal).
- Adjust expectations: translate, segment contexts, or pace for the lower literacy.

## micro-skills
- Identify the 'central question' being asked right now.
- Spot era-mismatch cues (awakening language vs piloting language).
- Offer a translation move (plant→animal or animal→plant) without flattening either.
- Recommend segmentation (public vs private, beginner vs advanced) when appropriate.

## local definitions
- **plant era:** capacity/awakening oriented interaction domain.
- **animal era:** navigation/piloting oriented interaction domain.
- **era mismatch:** participants assume different era literacies.
