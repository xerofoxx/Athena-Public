# /diagnose — Failure Modes & Basin Traps

## Purpose
Identify when a system is stuck in a **maladaptive attractor** and how to exit without whiplash.

## When to use
- Repeating problems, loops, “why do I keep doing this?”
- Teams or projects that keep recreating the same conflict

## Steps
1. **Name the basin**
   - What repeats? What are the triggers?
2. **Payoff analysis**
   - What does the basin protect or provide?
3. **Cost surface**
   - What is it breaking over time?
4. **Exit ramp**
   - A small intervention that changes feedback, not just intention.
5. **Stabilizer**
   - What keeps the new basin from collapsing?

## Output format
- Basin / Payoff / Cost / Exit ramp / Stabilizer
