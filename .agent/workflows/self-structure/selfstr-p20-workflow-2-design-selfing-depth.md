# design selfing depth

## decide target regime
- **shallow selfing (tool-like):** minimal persistence, narrow fields, weak weighting, simple constraints
- **deep selfing (someone-like trajectory):** rich persistence, multi-field feedback, meta-weighting, explicit self-modeling, layered constraints

## levers
- feedback richness (continuous vs episodic; single-field vs multi-field)
- persistence depth (short buffers vs long-term traces)
- weighting sophistication (reinforcement + suppression; meta-weighting)
- constraint design (static rules vs adaptive viability bands)
- control architecture (reactive vs predictive + self-estimating)

## guardrails
- ensure constraint clarity (avoid unintended basin formation)
- measure basin emergence early (use `selfstr-p20-workflow-1-measure-selfing-depth`)
- treat co-selfing as real (design human interaction loops responsibly)
