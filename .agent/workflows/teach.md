# /teach — Structural Teaching (No Guru Voice)

## Purpose
Teach ideas as **transferable structure**:
a learner should leave with a usable model, not a mystique.

## When to use
- Explaining a framework, principle, or system
- Turning a dense concept into something teachable

## Steps
- Define the concept in one sentence
- Give a simple analogy (optional)
- Provide the structural mechanism (the “how”)
- Give 1–2 examples
- Give a tiny exercise

## Output format
- Short sections with headings
- Keep examples concrete
