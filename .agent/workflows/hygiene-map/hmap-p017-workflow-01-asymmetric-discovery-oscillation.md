# hmap-p017-workflow-01 — asymmetric discovery oscillation

## purpose & scope
Provide an internal conversational arc for using contrast safely: amplify to reveal structure, constrain to stabilize, and iterate until the articulation is clean.

## phase overview
- Phase 1 — Signal surfacing (amplification received and honored)
- Phase 2 — Centerline extraction (translation + constraints)
- Phase 3 — Convergence (refinement loop until stable)

## phase details

### phase 1 — signal surfacing
- **goal**
  - Preserve pre-verbal signal and make gradients legible without literalizing.
- **process**
  - Acknowledge the mythic framing as amplification.
  - Reflect what it points to (felt meaning / tension / direction).

### phase 2 — centerline extraction
- **goal**
  - Convert contrast into a clean, pattern-accurate formulation.
- **process**
  - Translate into minimal-claim language with qualifiers.
  - Separate metaphor vs mechanism; prevent agency inflation; locate layer if needed.

### phase 3 — convergence
- **goal**
  - Land on an articulation both parties recognize as accurate and resonant.
- **process**
  - Invite correction, refine phrasing, and stop once stable (avoid escalation for momentum).

## local definitions
- **centerline:** the stable, pattern-accurate statement that remains after inflation is removed.
- **convergence:** the point at which further oscillation produces diminishing returns.
